The Battle Ship Metaphor

I am henry. I write this shitpost to explain how blink's core mechanic works (the battleship game).

Wtf is that?

If you are a newborn or a 10 yo with autism, battle ship is simply a game where you have to shoot down enemy's battleship without knowing where their battleship is.

So that "where" for blink is basically a process to locate the path to skip walls or any obstruction during the path finding process to the final destination.

Imagine the server and your game as enemy and the player respectively. Like irl battleship game, there's a wall that distants the 2 sides out. On Hypixel,

that wall is the server preventing the client from knowing how to go to the destination without getting obstructed. However, what if you add

a spectator to the irl battleship game? Well, you now have a middleman to help you know where the enemy's ships are. Likewise, NOCOM helps blink know where

to go without bonking your head into the wall or getting lagged back. So yeah, that's it, you can dm @zzzdeefer for more details or go on x perm's github for the nitty gritty nerdy shit i won't be bother.
